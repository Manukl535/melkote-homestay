Terms & Conditions

1. Introduction

Welcome to our homestay. By securing a booking, you agree to comply with the house rules and policies outlined below to ensure a pleasant stay for everyone.

2. Booking & Cancellation Policy

We strive to be flexible, but strict timelines apply to ensure fairness:

  • Occupancy: Maximum 6 guests allowed per booking (Ideal for 4 Adults + 2 Children).
  • Confirmation: Full payment is required at the time of booking to block your dates.
  • Refunds: Cancellations made more than 24 hours before check-in are eligible for a full refund.
  • Late Cancellation: Cancellations made within 24 hours of check-in are non-refundable.

3. Payment Policy

We exclusively accept digital payments to ensure a seamless and documented transaction. Accepted methods include:

  • Unified Payments Interface (UPI)
  • Google Pay (GPay)
  • PhonePe
  • Paytm

Note: We do not accept credit cards or cash on arrival.

4. Privacy Policy

Your privacy is paramount. Personal details (such as Aadhar numbers) are collected solely for government compliance and guest verification. We do not share your data with third-party advertisers.

Guests are responsible for their personal belongings. The management is not liable for any loss or damage. We reserve the right to evict guests who violate community guidelines or cause property damage.
